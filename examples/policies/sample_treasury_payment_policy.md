# Treasury Same-Day Payment Policy

Portfolio PORT_TREASURY_OPS_440 uses the same-day treasury cash movement
workflow for USD settlement support.

All same-day funding requirements for this operating window total $210 million.
The treasury cutoff hour is 15:00 New York time. Source accounts must retain a
minimum buffer of $30 million after approved transfers. Payment rail transfer
limits must not exceed $150 million for a single transfer through Fedwire or
CHIPS.

During liquidity stress, apply a funding stress multiplier of 115%. Materiality
notional for governance review is $200 million.
